## Overview

DiscordLink+ is a Minecraft plugin by [SushiMC](https://github.com/codingsushi79) that bridges your server with Discord — similar to [DiscordSRV](https://github.com/DiscordSRV/DiscordSRV) — with account linking and **Simple Voice Chat** group bridging.
